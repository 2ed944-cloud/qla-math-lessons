Part 3 — GCF & LCM

Learning Outcomes (P3)

  • Compute GCF and LCM by listing, prime exponents, or Euclid’s algorithm.
  • Choose the most efficient method for the number size.
  • Verify that \(ab=\mathrm{GCF}(a,b)\cdot \mathrm{LCM}(a,b)\) for two numbers.

Bridge from Part 2

Prime exponents summarize numbers. GCF = minimum exponents; LCM = maximum exponents.
